Session 3

Intro

Today, our adventure begins in a very special place, the aquatic ecosystem! This is a world full of life beneath the surface of lakes, rivers, and oceans. But before we dive into the water and meet the creatures that live there, we need to understand something very important: our role as inhabitants of this ecosystem.

As humans, our actions can have a big impact on the aquatic world around us. So, let’s start by learning about how we, as part of nature, can help protect and care for this amazing environment. Are you ready to explore the underwater world and discover how we can make a difference?

Let’s begin our adventure!

Today we will read a text, but first, pay attention to the instructions.
