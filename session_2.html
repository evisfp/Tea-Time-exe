Session 2

Intro

Today, our adventure begins in an amazing place — the terrestrial ecosystem, the world we live in! As inhabitants of this planet, it's important to understand how our actions affect the environment around us. We will start by exploring how we, as humans, are part of this ecosystem and how our choices can either help or harm the Earth. Get ready to learn about the delicate balance that keeps everything alive and how we can make a difference in protecting it. Let the adventure begin!

We are going to continue with yesterday´s lesson, but today we will talk about human actions! Are you ready? Let´s go.

During the presentation you will be asked the following questions:

  1. What human activities have shaped the environment? 
  2. have these activities affected the ecosystem for the better or the worse? 
  3. What transportations harms the environment? 
  4. Does someone know what “overfishing” is? 
  5. What is climate change? 
  6. How do you think we can reduce the environmental effects of the human activities we discussed today?
